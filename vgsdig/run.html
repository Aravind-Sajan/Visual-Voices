Run

To sign run, position both lateral L hands in the sign for fast then hook them together, one hand in front of the other. Do a fast-forward, pointer-wriggling motion with the leading hand that conveys movement.
















How to use

Run is a wonderful sign for toddlers. Use it during games as a contrast for walking, jumping or coming to a full stop . Treat yourself to something you enjoy straight after you run or move, so your brain can associate exercise with an immediate reward
